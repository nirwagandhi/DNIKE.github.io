Jordan 1 Retro High Bloodline


Rp.4.500.000

Jordan 1 Retro High Bloodline


Jordan Brand has big plans for the ultra-popular Air Jordan 1 during the holiday season, and one piece of the multi-shoe puzzle is the Air Jordan 1 Retro High OG “Bloodline.” Featuring a reimagined take on a familiar color scheme and inspired by a famous Air Jordan ad from 1985 that showed Michael Jordan soaring through the air for a death-defying dunk, the “Bloodline” is a fresh nod to an early piece of brand history.

Dressed in the popular Chicago Bulls color scheme. This Air Jordan 1 features White on the tongues, sides, toe box, and midsole with Black overlays. Red trimming throughout the entire shoe paired with matching Red laces and rubber outsole. Finishing off the design are “Nike Air” tongues and “Who Said Man Was Not Meant To Fly” printed on the insoles.

Jordan 1 Retro High Dior


Rp.180.000.000

Jordan 1 Retro High Dior


For the Men’s Fall 2020 Runway show in Miami, Dior and Kim Jones partnered with Jordan Brand to unveil the limited-edition Air Jordan 1 High OG Dior sneaker. A collection of ready-to-wear and accessories completes the collaboration, and a low version of the Air Jordan 1 OG Dior rounds out the range.

The special AJ1 is made entirely in Italy featuring a mixture of white and Dior Gray leather, with an Oblique monogrammed Swoosh (similar in style to the graphic featured on the fashion house’s popular B23 high-top sneaker) and hand-painted edges. "Air Dior" has also been applied to Jordan's familiar Wings logo.
