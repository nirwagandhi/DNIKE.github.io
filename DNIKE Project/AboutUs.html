About Us

Description


DNike is one of the sneaker shops in Denpasar, Bali. DNike strives to provide its customers with lower prices than the market. DNike was founded in 2017 by its own CEO, James Bond. DNike sells various types of authentic basketball shoes. Currently DNike is designing a website to reach more customers and also opening branches outside Bali. And in the future DNike will release applications that are available on IOS and Android devices so that it is easier for customers to make transactions at our store.

CEO of DNike

James Bond

CEO of DNike

James Bond was born in 1990 on Bali. At the beginning he really likes to collect shoes, especially basketball shoes. he started collecting basketball shoes at the age of 20 years. that's where he got the inspiration to open a basketball shoe store. at first he trying to sell it on online marketplace. as time goes by, he opened the first physical store of DNike in 2017 on Denpasat, Bali.
